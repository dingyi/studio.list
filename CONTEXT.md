# studio.list

studio.list is a visual directory for discovering design agencies, inspecting concise profiles, and continuing to their official websites.

## Language

**Agency**:
An organization or studio offering design or creative services, represented as one discoverable entry in the directory and identified by its Official Domain.
_Avoid_: Studio, vendor, provider

**Official Domain**:
The normalized domain of an Agency's official website, used to distinguish one Agency from another and merge duplicate source records.
_Avoid_: Display URL, Agency name, social profile

**Agency Discovery**:
The visitor's primary activity of visually browsing agencies and choosing one they want to inspect or visit.
_Avoid_: Ranking, reviewing, marketplace shopping

**Agency Candidate**:
An imported source record awaiting identity, link, and screenshot validation before it may become a Published Agency.
_Avoid_: Published Agency, listing, placeholder

**Published Agency**:
An Agency whose name and official link are valid, whose website is reachable, and for which an authentic website screenshot has been captured successfully.
_Avoid_: Candidate, broken listing, placeholder listing

**Agency Screenshot**:
An authentic, unobstructed desktop above-the-fold capture of an Agency's official homepage, used as its primary visual representation. Captures dominated by consent, regional, subscription, or similar overlays require review before they qualify.
_Avoid_: Logo, portfolio image, full-page capture, placeholder image

**Agency Description**:
An Agency's source-provided slogan or summary, preserved in its original language and meaning with formatting corrections only.
_Avoid_: AI summary, translation, inferred service claims

**Agency Location**:
One or more normalized countries where an Agency is based, displayed with an emoji flag and English country name.
_Avoid_: Raw flag string, inferred location, service market

**Screenshot Refresh**:
The replacement of an Agency Screenshot after an Agency is added, its official link changes, or a refresh is explicitly requested.
_Avoid_: Page build, automatic periodic recapture

**Agency Detail**:
The directory's dedicated profile for one Published Agency, reached through the main body of its card. It presents a larger visual preview and verified facts without inventing services, clients, work, or awards.
_Avoid_: Official website, review page

**Official Website Action**:
A distinct action on an Agency card or Agency Detail that opens the Agency's official website without passing through another directory page.
_Avoid_: Card action, detail navigation
